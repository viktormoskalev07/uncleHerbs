about us

Uncle Herbs is an independently black owned business whose brand is built around the feeling of trust.

Uncle Herbs aims to be that guiding light of wisdom for cannabis consumers amongst the misinformation, prior negative consumption experiences, a multitude of products, and overall stigmas around cannabis.

We are ultimately focused around the positive advancement of legal cannabis consumption through our pillars of education, community, sustainability, and diversity. We want our Herbie customers to come, and feel at home... Don't forget to support local!
